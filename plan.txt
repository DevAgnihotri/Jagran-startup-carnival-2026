## **Introduction & Aim of the Event**

Jagran Incubation & Entrepreneurship Cell (JIEC), JIM, in collaboration with I-Tech Innovation Foundation, presents:

**“Business Leadership & Entrepreneurial Conclave 2026: From Vision to Venture.”**

This two-day event will bring together entrepreneurs, innovators, and industry experts to promote:

* Entrepreneurial thinking
* Leadership skills
* Networking opportunities

Key highlights include:

* Launch of **Family Business & Sustainable Entrepreneurship Programme**
* Startup exhibitions
* Panel discussions
* “Jagranpreneurs 2026” – Startup pitching competition
* Mentorship and growth opportunities
